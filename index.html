ABOUT HAVANA

Havana Company is one of the major sellers of tobacco products throughout the USA.

With several tobacco factories and rich history, for 50 years we have been manufacturing and supplying the best cigars and premium tobacco to America. We sell our cigars to the US shops of any size and to individuals through the chain of Havana Tobacco stores.

HISTORY

For over 50 years, we have been engaged in tobacco growing and the production of cigars and other tobacco products.

SEPT 19, 1970

Foundation of our first tobacco factory in Havana by three brothers: James, John and Hugh Bomstrom.

JUN 2, 1990

Rise to the market leader in the USA, the opening of the first official store and 3 more factories.

NOV 17, 2000

Signing a contract with Trader Joe’s. Since then, we've become a regular supplier to all its stores.

MAY 5, 2015

Establishment of the fifth tobacco factory in Cuba. Market launch of 3 new cigar brands.

GOOD CIGARS START WITH GOOD TOBACCO

We have gained an excellent reputation in the tobacco market. Why? Because we are responsible for what we do.

FROM SEED TO CIGAR
Our modern and well-equipped cigar factories in Cuba do not rely solely on technologies. On the contrary, the making of cigars still requires human skills. Our employees are engaged in a craft and are supported by modern technologies.

QUALITY THAT CAN'T BE OVERESTIMATED
Before a wrapper is wound around a cigar, it passes through at least three hundred pairs of hands. You can trace the journey of your cigar from the tobacco leaf or even seed to the boxed cigar. We use the most modern approaches and only natural ingredients.

THE ORIGIN OF OUR TOBACCOS

All our plantations are located in Cuba in the vicinity of Havana.

The quality of our tobacco is determined by the composition of the seeds, the soil, climatic conditions, the curing and fermentation processes and the skill with which the tobacco is handled. As tobacco is a natural product, its quality and flavor can differ from one year to another. The country and the region also matter. The tobacco produced in Cuba is known for its aromatic properties.

OUR BRANDS

We sell tobacco products under several subsidiary brands. Look for these brands in stores in your city.

FINE CIGAR

Imported from Cuba, these machine-made cigars use select natural leaf wrappers and aged filler tobaccos to emphasize their nice flavor and aroma. Produced since 2000, this brand included in the Havana family has a huge following among American smokers.

CIGAR WORLD

Handmade in Cuba, the Cigar World cigars display the finest aged binder and filler tobaccos surrounded by natural wrapper for great flavor with that special Havana touch. The rich tobacco sweetness is accentuated with hearty notes of dark roast coffee.

CIGAR BAR

Cigar Bar cigarillos are hand-rolled in Cuba with natural leaf wrappers. Choose the Aroma version dipped in cognac, the Sweet Filtered cognac-dipped or the strong Rum version. Allow yourself an evening with good tobacco.

OUR FOUNDERS

50 years ago, three brothers bought land in Cuba and organized the first tobacco plantation and the first Havana factory.

JOHN BOMSTROM

John and his brothers were from a poor family. John was the first to come to Cuba in search of better fortune and discovered the amazing properties of local tobacco.

JACK BOMSTROM

Jack took care of all the issues of managing the new enterprise since he graduated from the law faculty. Thanks to Jack, the Havana company still thrives.

HUGH BOMSTROM

Hugh started developing Havana brand stores and opening them in different states of the USA. Most of our official stores are still open since 1970 and have a rich history.
